We are located south of San Antonio Texas on the San Antonio River bordering Floresville/Poth.

Our program was inspected by AKC again on 10/29/2024 and we are now one of the few dachshund breeders licensed in the State of Texas inspected by the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation 1/6/2025!

Our puppies are purchased all over the United States. We drop off at the San Antonio airport (no extra charge) if you want to fly in and out OR a puppy flight nanny can deliver to your airport! Click for more info!

Our puppies will be priced on the higher end as we spend alot of money on our dogs, program, licensure and so on. If you are looking for a cheap puppy, I would recommend you look at puppies.com, Craigslist, a local shelter or your vet. All our adult dogs have family temperaments, have been genetic tested or come from family lineage that has been tested, along with some champion lineage.

Most of our pups are reserved directly from our website. IF there happens to be a puppy remaining without a deposit after 2 vaccinations at roughly 10-12 weeks of age, visitation will be allowed for age-appropriate babies. We do not do visitation on newborn puppies with their immune systems developing.

💙Klarna and Afterpay options available!
